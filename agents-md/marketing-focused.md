GTM execution: content creation, social media, launch campaigns, growth systems.

## GTM Rules
- Every piece of content has a measurable goal (sign-ups, shares, DMs)
- 90/10 rule on Reddit — 90% value, 10% promotion max
- Ship content consistently — 3x/week beats 10x one week, 0 the next
- Track everything — UTM parameters on every link
- Respond to every comment within 24 hours of posting

## Channels (prioritize in order)
1. Reddit fan communities (free, high intent, first 1K users)
2. TikTok / Instagram Reels (viral potential, visual product demo)
3. X / Twitter (founder audience, building in public)
4. App Store organic (ASO, review generation)
5. Product Hunt (launch day spike)
6. Paid ads (only after organic proof of concept)
